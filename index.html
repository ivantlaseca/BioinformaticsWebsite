KSU CCSE COHB

Bioinformatics and Biomedical Research Group

Advancing computing technology to improve health care

Dr. Aledhari's projects use artificial intelligence/machine learning (AI/ML) and computer vision (CV) techniques for bioinformatics and biomedical projects, including but not limited to computational biology, healthcare, medicine, biotechnology, genomics, drug discovery, and personalized medicine. Such implementations and analysis can lead to the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of diseases with genetic predisposition such as cancer, obesity, diabetes, heart disease, and mental illness. COHB seeks creative research focus students with solid analytical capabilities and versatile writing skills applicable to promptly developing websites. Dr. Aledhari’s research group is committed to conducting research in computational, data, and engineering sciences in the context of biomedical, clinical, and public health disciplines and applying this knowledge to support the nation’s leading health initiatives
